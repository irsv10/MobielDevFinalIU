    Digital Technology

    Digital ideas that solve real-world problems.

    We are 300,000 people collaborating across oceans and industries, and Digital Technology is the glue that holds us together. From high-profile corporate projects, to real-time work out in the field—digital at GE is all about finding creative ways of connecting technology, machines and people to make the world work better.

    Program Summary

    This leadership program is a two-year program with four rotational job assignments focused on GE’s top digital initiatives. Program members lead by doing—gaining real-world experience by working on important and challenging projects alongside some of the brightest minds in the world. Digital technology is the foundation for GE as a Digital Industrial company. Digital technology combines the best of software & information technology (IT) and is critical to the future of GE. With more than 11,000 professionals in roughly 50 countries, digital technology is a major area of focus and investment across the company and is essential to maintaining our leadership, competitiveness and success. For those with a passion for technology, no other company offers the diversity of experience and career potential that GE does. As a pipeline for future leaders of GE’s world-class global digital technology organization, our Digital Technology Leadership Program has partnered with academic and industry training providers to deliver continued education in technology, management and leadership. This training includes in-person classes inside and outside of the U.S., and other virtual electives.

    Candidate Criteria

    A Bachelor’s Degree in CIS, Computer Engineering, Computer Science, IT Security, ITM, Informatics, Information Science, Information Systems, Information Technology, MIS, Security and Risk Analysis or relevant STEM majors with experience in Software or IT experience or a minimum of 4 years of experience in the same areas.

    • Minimum cumulative 3.0/4.0 GPA or equivalent.
    • Strong analytical and technical skills
    • Must be georaphically mobile as requested
    • A strong commitment to a career in technology and passion/aptitude for both software and information technology.
    • Up to date with current industry standards and trending software and information technology development tools.

    GE is an equal opportunity employer. Legal authorization to work in the country/region where the business is hiring is required. We will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job.
